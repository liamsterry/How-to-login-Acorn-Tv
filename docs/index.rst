Troubleshooting Login Issues
-----------------------------

If you are unable to log in, try the following solutions:

Forgot Password
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Click on **“Forgot Password?”** on the login page.  
- Enter your registered email address.  
- Follow the password reset instructions sent to your email.  

Incorrect Email or Password
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Double-check for typing errors.  
- Ensure Caps Lock is turned off.  
- Try resetting your password if necessary.  

App Not Working
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Update the app to the latest version.  
- Restart your device.  
- Reinstall the Acorn TV app.  

Account Subscription Issues
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If your subscription has expired, you may need to renew it before accessing content.
